# Brain Dump - ADHD Daily Planner

## How to host this for free (GitHub Pages)

You need a GitHub account (free). That's it.

### Step by step:

1. Go to github.com and sign up (or log in)
2. Click the + button top right, then "New repository"
3. Name it: brain-dump
4. Make sure "Public" is selected
5. Click "Create repository"
6. On the next page, click "uploading an existing file"
7. Drag all 3 files from this folder (index.html, manifest.json, icon.svg) into the upload area
8. Click "Commit changes"
9. Go to Settings (tab at the top of your repo)
10. In the left sidebar, click "Pages"
11. Under "Source", select "Deploy from a branch"
12. Under "Branch", select "main" and "/ (root)"
13. Click Save
14. Wait about 60 seconds, then refresh the page
15. You'll see a link like: https://yourusername.github.io/brain-dump/

That's your app. It's live. Forever. Free.

### Add to your iPhone:
1. Open that link in Safari on your iPhone
2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. It'll appear as an app icon called "Brain Dump"

### Add to your Mac:
- Safari: File > Add to Dock
- Chrome: Three dots menu > Save and Share > Create Shortcut > tick "Open as window"

### Your data:
Everything saves to your browser's localStorage on each device.
Your iPhone and Mac will have separate task lists (they don't sync).
Use the Export button to download a daily backup as a text file.
